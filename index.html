Description

Fight against hordes of monsters and customize your skills in Waifu Survivors. Explore varied maps, progress with unique characters, and face epic battles. Get ready for an exciting journey!

History

Waifu Survivors - Survive with your waifus.

A terrible creature, which had been sealed away for ages in a forgotten place, has awakened and plunged the realms of this world into unimaginable chaos. Now, the forces of evil spread like a plague, threatening everything we know. Each character embarks on a unique journey, driven by their own motivations and secrets, to confront this imminent danger and restore order.

Chaos and Monsters

Prepare to face a series of fearsome monsters and challenging bosses throughout the story, including the monstrosity that sparked this entire era of chaos. With each battle, you'll encounter new challenges and opportunities to test your skills and strategy.

Tailor-Made Challenge

You can adjust the difficulty of the enemies to suit your taste: increase it if you're looking for a more intense and thrilling challenge, or decrease it if you prefer to focus on the story and exploration. Customize the difficulty according to your preferences and skills.

Create Your Combat Style

Experiment with a multitude of combinations between inherited skills from each heroine and other customizable abilities. Acquire new spells and attributes throughout your playthroughs, creating a unique experience each time and adapting your combat style to your preferences.

Hidden Stories and Destinies

Uncover the hidden story behind the creature threatening this world, understand the reason for the rise of so many monster hordes, and reveal the purpose of our heroines. As you complete the scenarios, the narrative about this world and its warriors unfolds, allowing you to get to know each of them and decide how their stories will conclude, shaping the future of their journeys.

Have fun, explore, and uncover the true essence of all this darkness.
